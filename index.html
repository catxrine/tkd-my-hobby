How it all started?

My family has been involved in Taekwon-do for many years, we have 2 black belts and a world champion. To be honest, I started working out even before I knew all this. I couldn't help but notice the beauty and discipline of this martial art.

Why Taekwon-do?

As you know, there are all kinds of martial arts and all of them are wonderful in their own ways. I have tried other martial arts, but I feel Taekwon-do as the closest to me

Where is the dojang?

The name of our club is Central Police Taekwondo Club (CPTK) Vratsa, open for all ages. Regular competitions and camps are held every year.

Other hobbies?

In general, I like everything related to Korean culture, I like to cook Korean food. Also, programming is one of the things I like to do and in which I want to develop.
